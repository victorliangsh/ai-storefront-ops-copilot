# Service Offer: AI Shopify Product Page Launch Pack

This document describes a practical service package that can be built from the AI Storefront Ops Copilot workflow.

The goal is to help Shopify and direct-to-consumer storefront operators turn a product idea into a clearer, more conversion-ready product page.

## Who This Service Is For

This service is designed for:

- Shopify store owners
- One-product storefront operators
- Solo founders testing a new product
- Small ecommerce teams without a dedicated copywriter
- Operators who need a faster product-page launch workflow

## Core Offer

AI Shopify Product Page Launch Pack

This package helps a merchant prepare the key product-page assets needed before sending traffic to a product page.

## Deliverables

A standard package can include:

- Product positioning summary
- Product title options
- Short product description
- Five product-page benefit bullets
- FAQ section
- Trust section near the purchase area
- Shipping and guarantee copy draft
- Storefront launch checklist
- Three short-form ad creative angles
- Daily operations review template

## Optional Add-ons

Additional services may include:

- Product selection scorecard
- Supplier review checklist
- Competitor landing-page structure review
- Storefront audit report
- Mobile product-page review
- Shopify product import draft
- AI prompt customization for the merchant's product category

## Client Input Needed

To prepare a useful launch pack, collect:

- Product name
- Product photos or supplier page
- Target customer
- Main customer problem
- Product benefits
- Price range
- Shipping notes
- Return or guarantee policy
- Store URL if available
- Any competitor references

## Workflow

1. Collect product and storefront information.
2. Score the product using the product selection framework.
3. Draft product-page copy and FAQ.
4. Create the trust section and launch checklist.
5. Review the mobile buying experience.
6. Prepare short-form ad creative angles.
7. Deliver the final Markdown or document package.

## Positioning Statement

AI-assisted Shopify product-page preparation for small ecommerce operators who need practical launch assets without hiring a full marketing team.

## Important Notes

This service should not promise guaranteed sales, conversion rates, or advertising performance.

The goal is to improve launch readiness, page clarity, and operating speed. Merchants should still validate product demand, supplier quality, payment readiness, shipping times, and compliance requirements before spending heavily on traffic.
