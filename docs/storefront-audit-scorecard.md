# Storefront Audit Scorecard

Use this scorecard before sending paid traffic to a Shopify or direct-to-consumer product page. It is designed for a quick AI-assisted review, not as a guarantee of sales or conversion results.

## When to Use

Use the scorecard when you want to decide whether a product page is ready for launch, needs a focused revision, or should be paused before spending more ad budget.

Recommended inputs:

- Product page URL or draft copy
- Product name and target customer
- Offer, price, shipping, and return policy
- Screenshots of the first screen on desktop and mobile
- Any available customer reviews or proof assets

## Score Categories

Score each category from 1 to 5.

| Category | What to Check | Score |
| --- | --- | --- |
| First-screen clarity | The visitor can understand what the product is and why it matters within 5 seconds. | 1-5 |
| Customer problem clarity | The page clearly names the pain point, desire, or use case. | 1-5 |
| Offer strength | Price, bundle, discount, guarantee, or bonus feels easy to understand. | 1-5 |
| Product proof | The page includes demos, reviews, before/after examples, UGC, or clear evidence. | 1-5 |
| Trust signals | Shipping, returns, payment security, brand contact, and store credibility are visible. | 1-5 |
| FAQ completeness | The page answers the most likely objections before checkout. | 1-5 |
| Mobile buying flow | Mobile layout is readable and the add-to-cart path is obvious. | 1-5 |
| Checkout readiness | Variants, quantities, price, shipping notes, and checkout actions are not confusing. | 1-5 |
| Shipping and return clarity | Delivery timing, return rules, and support expectations are stated plainly. | 1-5 |
| Next action clarity | The primary button and next step are visible, direct, and consistent. | 1-5 |

Maximum score: 50

## Decision Guide

| Total Score | Suggested Decision |
| --- | --- |
| 40-50 | Ready for a small traffic test. Track conversion and customer questions. |
| 30-39 | Fix the top 3 weak areas before increasing traffic. |
| 20-29 | Rewrite the page structure and offer before launch. |
| Below 20 | Do not send paid traffic yet. Rebuild the positioning, proof, and buying flow. |

## Example AI Prompt

```text
You are an ecommerce storefront auditor. Review the product page using the Storefront Audit Scorecard. Score each category from 1 to 5, explain the reason for each score, then give the top 3 fixes before paid traffic. Do not promise revenue or conversion results. Focus on clarity, trust, proof, offer strength, and mobile buying flow.
```

## Notes

This scorecard is intended to help operators make better launch decisions. It should be combined with real store analytics, customer feedback, ad performance data, and compliance review when needed.
