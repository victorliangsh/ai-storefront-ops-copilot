# Use Cases

AI Storefront Ops Copilot focuses on practical workflows for Shopify and direct-to-consumer storefront operators.

## 1. Product Selection Research

Operators can use structured scorecards to compare products before building a store.

Example evaluation points:

- Clear visual demo
- Simple customer problem
- Easy supplier sourcing
- Healthy pricing margin
- Low return and compliance risk
- Strong short-form content potential

## 2. Shopify Product Page Copy

The project provides templates for generating:

- Product titles
- Product descriptions
- Bullet points
- FAQ sections
- Trust sections
- Shipping and guarantee copy

## 3. Storefront Launch Workflow

Small sellers often need a repeatable launch process.

This project documents a practical workflow for:

- Product research
- Supplier review
- Product-page creation
- Offer structure
- Storefront checklist
- Launch readiness review

## 4. Storefront Audit

Operators can use checklist-based reviews to improve product pages before spending money on ads.

Audit areas include:

- First-screen clarity
- Offer strength
- Product benefits
- Trust signals
- Mobile layout
- Checkout readiness

## 5. Daily Operations Review

The project includes templates for daily ecommerce reviews.

A daily report may include:

- Sales and traffic summary
- Product-page issues
- Ad creative notes
- Customer questions
- Storefront improvement tasks
- Next-day action list

## 6. AI Workflow Templates

The long-term goal is to provide reusable AI prompts and small tools that help operators move faster without needing a large ecommerce team.
