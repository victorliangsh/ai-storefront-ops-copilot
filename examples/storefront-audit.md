# Storefront Audit Example

This example shows how AI Storefront Ops Copilot can review a one-product storefront before traffic is sent to the page.

## Storefront Context

Store type: One-product Shopify storefront  
Product category: Pet grooming tool  
Traffic source: Short-form video ads and organic social content  
Goal: Improve first-screen clarity, trust, and product-page conversion readiness  

## Audit Summary

The storefront should quickly answer three questions:

- What is the product?
- What problem does it solve?
- Why should the visitor trust this store?

If these answers are not clear within the first few seconds, visitors may leave before reading the full product page.

## First-Screen Review

### What Works

- Product is visually simple and easy to understand
- Customer problem is common and easy to recognize
- The product has strong short-form video potential
- A one-product page is a good fit for fast testing

### What To Improve

- Make the main benefit visible near the add-to-cart button
- Add a short trust section close to the product purchase area
- Use clearer before-and-after grooming language
- Make mobile buttons and variant selection easy to see
- Avoid long paragraphs above the buying section

## Product Page Checklist

### Above The Fold

- Clear product name
- Strong hero image or product demo
- Simple benefit statement
- Visible price
- Clear add-to-cart button
- Shipping or guarantee message near the purchase area

### Trust Signals

- Secure checkout message
- Shipping information
- Return or satisfaction policy
- Product usage notes
- Customer support contact path

### Mobile Experience

- Buttons should be easy to tap
- Variant selection should be visually clear
- Text should not be too dense
- Product benefits should appear before long descriptions
- The page should avoid horizontal scrolling

## Recommended Improvements

1. Add a concise benefit block near the product button.
2. Add a short FAQ section below the main product description.
3. Improve mobile variant selection visibility.
4. Place shipping and guarantee notes closer to the buying area.
5. Use video or GIF-style product demonstration when possible.

## Example Trust Block

Simple home grooming for shedding season.

- Helps manage loose fur at home
- Designed for cats and dogs
- Easy to use as part of a regular grooming routine
- Useful between professional grooming sessions
